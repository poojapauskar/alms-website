Fairy tale can shape a mind, without the mind knowing it.

It gives so much importance on physical appearance of a woman that she often feel the need to change in order to get a man to like her.

A woman in trouble is rescued by a man who keeps her happy ever after. Thus a woman often set unrealistic expectation from man and feel dependent while the man feel more dominant than woman.

Fairy tale do influence in long run.
