Fairy tale can shape a mind, without the mind knowing it.

Fairy tale give so much importance on physical appearance of a girl that it reflects the idea of how women often feel the need to change in order to get a man to like her.

In fairy tale the prince is the ideal one who comes to the rescue of a princess in distress and promise to keep her happy ever after. It reflects the idea of how woman feel dependent on man and man feel more powerful than woman. It reflects the idea of how woman feel the need to be complete and happy by a man.
