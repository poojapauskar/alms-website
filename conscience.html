Its only me and my conscience

I am answerable only to my conscience. It knows the whole story. Behind every action, it knows the reasons, intentions, hesitations, fears, efforts and mistakes.

It will see efforts, rather than seing outcomes. It will see growth, rather than mistakes. It will see reason and intention, and not judge.

In that space, right and wrong is felt. It allows to sense them and when i ignore the wrong, there is no outward punishment, but it will distant me from myself, weighing more heavily upon me.

When i know i have to take action, but stay silent to avoid being judged, I may keep peace outside, but create conflict within.

The world can applaud you and it still wont feel like peace. I need not please anyone. I need not defend any step. I know i need to act and not just reflect.

And at the end, all i need is to sit with myself and if my conscience is at peace, I know Im being true.
