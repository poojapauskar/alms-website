Fairy tale can shape a mind, without the mind knowing it.

It gives too much importance on physical appearance of a woman. Thus woman often feel the need to change in order to get a man to like her.

A woman is shown in trouble and she is rescued by a man who keeps her happy ever after. Thus woman feel dependent on man to complete her and set unrealistic expectation in relation. While the man feel dominant over woman.

Inner beauty and growing through imperfection is more beautiful.
